BEFORE YOU START!
 · Heavy! Lifting requires 4 people. The PIXL Dark  weighs >100kg. It will require 
4 people to lift it from the bench onto the packing crate base. Always lift the 
PIXL Dark by it’s side transit handles. To reduce manual handling, ensure 
the packing crate base is close to the PIXL Dark crate when you are ready to 
perform the lift. Leave at least 1m around the crate base to comfortably pack 
the PIXL Dark.
